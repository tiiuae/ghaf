Decision Record Template

Section titled “Decision Record Template”

This is the template1 for managing the ADR files. Use the following sections in each ADR file:

Title

Section titled “Title”

Status

Section titled “Status”

What is the status? Proposed, Accepted, Rejected, Deprecated, Superseded, etc.

Context

Section titled “Context”

What is the issue that we are seeing that is motivating this decision or change?

Decision

Section titled “Decision”

What is the change that we are proposing and/or doing?

Consequences

Section titled “Consequences”

What becomes easier or more difficult to do because of this change?
